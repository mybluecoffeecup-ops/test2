Newbie FAQ

Before Your First Paddle

Everything first-timers ask us, answered straight.

I've never paddled before — can I join?

Absolutely. Most of our paddlers started with zero experience. All nationalities, fitness levels and genders are welcome (ages 15+), our coaches teach the basics from stroke one, and regular Newbie Days are built exactly for first-timers.

How do I sign up for a session?

Start with the trial package — two dragon boat sessions. Send us the enquiry form or message us on WhatsApp and we'll point you at the next session.

Where do we meet?

Dragon boat sessions run from the Kallang Water Sports Centre, 10 Stadium Lane — a short walk from Stadium MRT. Outrigger sessions launch from Sentosa. Aim to arrive 10–15 minutes early so the crew hits the water on time.

What should I bring?

The training essentials:

  • Big bottle of water
  • Sunscreen
  • Sporty clothes, a hat and flip-flops
  • Change of dry clothes
Do I need to be able to swim?

For dragon boat, personal flotation devices are available, so confident swimming isn't a hard requirement. For outrigger, a swim test without a PFD is mandatory — be honest with yourself about your water confidence, and check the weather and tide forecast before sessions.

What does a session look like?

A group warm-up on land, technique work, then the bulk of the session on the water — around 1.5 to 2 hours all up, scaled to the crew on the day.

How do I meet people at the club?

Stick around after training — the crew usually grabs food or drinks together — and watch for member-only socials and events at our partner venues. Train hard, play hard.
